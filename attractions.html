Featured Attractions

Explore Mysuru's most captivating destinations

Karanji Lake Serenity

A tranquil urban oasis perfect for birdwatching and peaceful morning walks amidst nature.

Chamundi Hill

Panoramic hilltop views of Mysore city with spiritual significance and breathtaking sunsets.

Lingambudhi Lake Trail

Scenic walking trail offering serene lake views and a peaceful escape from city life.

Nanjangud Temple

Ancient temple known as the 'Kashi of the South' with stunning Dravidian architecture.

Mysore Palace

Mysore Palace, also known as Amba Vilas Palace, is a historical palace and a royal residence

Kukkarahalli Lake

Peaceful urban lake ideal for birdwatching and morning walks.

St. Philomena's Church

Neo-Gothic architectural marvel and peaceful place of worship.

Mysore Zoo

India's oldest zoo, known for its natural enclosures and conservation efforts.

Brindavan Gardens

Iconic musical fountain garden beside the KRS dam with spectacular light shows.

Jaganmohan Palace

Art gallery featuring royal collections and traditional paintings.

Devaraja Market

Historic marketplace full of local colors, flowers, and aromatic spices.

Chunchanakatte Falls

Hidden waterfall near Mysore with stunning monsoon flow and natural beauty.
